Our Vision

Beyond Bricks and Mortar

Parsifae Architecture Ltda operates at the unique intersection of architectural design and real estate brokerage. Led by Raphael Augusto Borges Vieira, our firm understands that buying a property is not just a financial transaction, but an investment in a lifestyle and an aesthetic legacy.

Based in the vibrant heart of Goiânia, Goiás, we distinguish ourselves from traditional brokers by offering an architect's eye to every viewing and negotiation. We don't just sell square footage; we evaluate structural integrity, design potential, and spatial harmony.

Whether you are an investor looking for high-yield developments or a homeowner seeking a residence with soul, Parsifae provides the technical expertise and market insight to secure your ideal asset. We believe every property has a narrative, and our job is to help you write the next chapter.

What We Do

Integrated Property Solutions

⚖

Architectural Brokerage

We specialize in the sale and acquisition of architecturally significant properties. Our team assesses the build quality, zoning potential, and design merit of listings, ensuring our clients invest in real estate that holds intrinsic value beyond the market trends.

✎

Design-Build Consulting

For clients purchasing land or fixer-uppers, we offer preliminary architectural consulting during the buying process. We help visualize renovations, estimate construction costs, and define the feasibility of your dream project before you sign the deed.

📈

Asset Management

We assist investors in managing real estate portfolios with a focus on value retention through maintenance and aesthetic upgrades. We identify properties in Goiás with high appreciation potential driven by urban planning and architectural development.
